What is the goal of the first pass when reading a paper?
To get a very general idea of the paper's structure and importance.
What are the main steps in the first pass?
Read the title, abstract, and introduction; skim headings and math content; read conclusion; scan references.

What is the purpose of skimming references during the first pass?
To identify familiar sources and evaluate the scholarly background of the paper.
What are the five Cs you should be able to answer after the first pass?
Category, Context, Correctness, Contributions, Clarity.
